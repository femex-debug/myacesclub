Our Mission

More Than Tennis

ACES Club is dedicated to promoting sportsmanship, community engagement, and an active lifestyle through tennis and other sports. We create a supportive environment for members of all ages and skill levels while extending our impact to African communities — providing essential resources such as clean water, food, and youth sports development programs.

Our Vision

Building a Legacy Through Sport

To be a leading community-driven sports organization that inspires active living, develops competitive athletes, and creates lasting positive change in underserved communities locally and across Africa through the power of sport.

Our History

How It Started

Founded in 2023 by Oluwafemi Agbayewa in Laurel, Maryland, ACES Club began as a small group of tennis enthusiasts who shared a passion for the game and a desire to give back. What started as casual weekend matches quickly grew into an organized club with structured tournaments, training programs, and community outreach initiatives. Today, ACES Club serves over 100 members and continues to expand its mission both locally and internationally.
